Staff Applications

The following is a list of questions required to submit an application to the Minecraft 3DS Community staff team. Response to all questions is required.

Staff applications are only open during windows announced in the Minecraft 3DS Community Discord server. Sending an application outside of a designated window will result in immediate rejection of your application, with no exceptions.

To submit your application, send your responses to the below questions to wyndchyme_ on Discord via private messages.


It is recommended to respond to questions 4-10 with at least one paragraph.

1. What is your Discord username?


2. What is your age?


3. How long have you been a member of the Minecraft 3DS Discord server?


4. Are you familiar with any programming languages? If so, which ones? What is your skill level?


5. How active are you on Discord? Will you maintain the same level of activity for the forseeable future?


6. Why did you join the Minecraft 3DS Community Discord server?


7. Why do you want to be a staff member?


8. What skills and experience would make you a good staff member?


9. Do you agree with the rules of the Minecraft 3DS Community Discord server? If not, what do you disagree with? How would you change them?


10. If you could change one thing about the server, what would it be?




Repeatedly asking about the status of your staff application may result in immediate rejection of your application.

You may be contacted for further questions by the staff team after submitting your application. If this occurs, please respond in a timely manner.

Results of the staff applications will be announced publicly in the Discord server's announcements channel.
